LEMON KKRUMBLE

LEMON KKRUMBLE RECIPE WITH HOME MADE STRAWBERRY JAM FOR THE BADDIES

MAKE IT WITH UR BESTY BADDIES

INGREDIENTS

  • 400G FLOUR
  • 120G BUTTER(SOFT)
  • 100G SUGAR
  • 8G BAKING POWDER
  • 2 EGGS
  • 400G JAM
  • LEMON ZEST
  • 1TBS VANILLA EXTRACT
  • PINCH OF SALT

STEPS

  1. ADD ALL POWDERS. MIX
  2. ADD EGGS, SOFTENED BUTTER, VANILLA EXTRACT, LEMON ZEST. MIX BY HAND TIL SANDY+CRUMBLY
  3. 180 DEGREES FOR 35-40 MIN
